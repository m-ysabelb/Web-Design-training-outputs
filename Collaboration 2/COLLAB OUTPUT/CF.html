Creative Vegetarian Cuisine

Since opening its doors, Cafe Flora has been at the forefront of utilizing local, organic and sustainable production with Washington farms. Its culinary team draws inspiration from international traditions and the availability of locally grown produce to create imaginative and dynamic vegetarian, gluten-free cuisine.

Meet the Owner

Nat Stratton-Clarke is the owner of the Flora Restaurant Group: Cafe Flora, The Flora Bakehouse, and Floret. He has fostered a haven for vegetarians and vegans alike for almost 30 years with local, organic, and sustainable goods with an eye on being eco-conscious and producing food that is simply delicious.
